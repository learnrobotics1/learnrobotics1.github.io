Overview

Robotics combines programming, electronics, and mechanics into a device that helps us perform tasks in the real-world. We will use the affordable Cubit STEAM Kit to complete interactive robotics labs.

Some questions to think about as you go through this lesson are:

  • How do we develop an educational lesson using robots?
  • What process steps do we need to take to achieve our outcome?
  • How can we modify our lesson to cater to new and advanced learners?

Goals

In this lesson, you’ll be given the opportunity to use the Cubit explorer kit to create solutions to the Lab Activities.

These are the goals you’ll achieve at the completion of this lesson.

  1. Learn how to teach the Engineering Design Process
  2. Understand how to teach students applied STEM
  3. Translate drawings & plans into a prototype
  4. Design lesson plans that you can use in your class
